### 🍽️ Zomasta – Food Reels Platform

A full-stack MERN web application where food partners can upload short food videos and users can explore, like, and save food reels — inspired by Instagram Reels but focused on food discovery.

---

### 🚀 Features
👤 User Registration & Login |
🏪 Food Partner Registration & Login |
🎥 Upload Food Reels (Video Upload) |
❤️ Like Food Videos |
🔖 Save Food Videos |
👨‍🍳 Food Partner Profile Page |
📱 Reels-style Vertical Video Feed |
🔐 JWT Authentication with Cookies |
☁️ Cloud Database (MongoDB Atlas) |
🖼️ Video Storage using ImageKit |
🌐 Fully Deployed (Frontend + Backend) |
